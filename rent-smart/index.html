Stage 1 — Rent Smart

Before buying a home, we build strong habits while renting.

1. Understand Your Lease

Always read your lease carefully. Understand rent due dates, maintenance responsibilities, renewal terms, and penalties for late payments.

2. Track Your Expenses

Keep a simple monthly record of rent, utilities, subscriptions, and other recurring costs. This builds awareness and prepares you for future mortgage planning.

3. Build an Emergency Fund

Even as a renter, unexpected expenses happen. Start with a small goal — $500 to $1,000 — and grow it gradually.
