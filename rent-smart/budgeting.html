Budgeting for Rent

Rent isn’t just a number. It’s part of a system.

The 30% Guideline

A common rule is to spend no more than 30% of your monthly income on rent.

It’s not a strict rule — but it helps prevent financial pressure.

Know Your Real Monthly Cost

  • Rent
  • Utilities
  • Internet
  • Renters insurance
  • Parking (if applicable)

Your “rent” is often higher than the listing price.

Simple Budget Structure

  • Needs (rent, food, utilities)
  • Savings
  • Flexible spending

Budgeting isn’t restriction. It’s deciding where your money goes before it disappears.

Quick Self-Check

  • ☐ I know my monthly take-home income
  • ☐ I’ve calculated my full housing cost
  • ☐ I know how much I can realistically afford
